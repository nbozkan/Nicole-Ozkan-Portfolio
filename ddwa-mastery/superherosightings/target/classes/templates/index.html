About

With rising popularity of superhero movies, there has been a heightened awareness of superheroes in our midst. The frequency of superhero (and supervillian) sightings is increasing at an alarming rate. The Hero Education and Relationship Organization (HERO) developed this website as a database to track the ever-increasing sightings occuring globally. Thank you for visiting our webiste and bringing recognition to HERO and all our heroes.
